# Shared Family Transition — 10 Minutes

**When:** After parent, Lāla–Lālī, and Kiśora–Kiśorī parallel sessions (minute 40–50 of program block, or immediately before Bhakti laboratory).

## Purpose

Reunite families with one shared insight and one shared practice — without re-teaching the full lesson.

## Flow

| Time | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 0–2 min | Bell; families sit together. Facilitator: "What changed today — our bodies or our understanding?" |
| 2–5 min | **One-line share:** Each subgroup teaches one thing (pre-assigned): Parents — BG 2.13 summary; Lāla–Lālī — costume relay; Kiśora–Kiśorī — one case insight |
| 5–8 min | **Family pair practice:** Parent and child complete one language rewrite together (from parent lesson) OR child shows thread craft / identity card |
| 8–10 min | **Sankalpa preview:** Lead facilitator reads [sankalpa.md](../sankalpa.md) opening; families whisper their chosen identity-pause trigger |

## All-age recall (call and response)

> **Leader:** I have a body.  
> **All:** I have a body.  
> **Leader:** I am Kṛṣṇa's servant.  
> **All:** I am Kṛṣṇa's servant.  
> **Leader:** This body is for service.  
> **All:** This body is for service.

## Bridge to Bhakti laboratory

"We will now sit together, notice the breath, and chant — the conscious self hearing Kṛṣṇa's name." → [bhakti-lab.md](../bhakti-lab.md)

## Bridge to Cycle 1 project

Invite families to contribute **one** optional gallery item this month (photo timeline strip, poem, pledge card) — see [project/MODULE-PROJECT-BRIEF.md](../project/MODULE-PROJECT-BRIEF.md). No public disclosure of private struggles required.

## Newcomer note

Host families help newcomers find materials and translate recall line if needed. Newcomers may observe transition silently.
