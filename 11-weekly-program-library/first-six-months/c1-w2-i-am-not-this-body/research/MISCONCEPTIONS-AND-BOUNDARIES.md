# C1-W2 Misconceptions and Boundaries

## What "I am not this body" does NOT mean

| Misconception | Correction | Source |
| --- | --- | --- |
| The body is unreal; care doesn't matter | Body is temporary, not imaginary; instrument for service | [BG 2.16](https://vedabase.io/en/library/bg/2/16/), KUTUMBA safeguarding |
| Pain is illusion; ignore it | Compassion and medical care required; philosophy does not cancel pain | Safeguarding policy |
| I am not responsible for bodily actions | Soul is responsible; driver chooses | [BG 2.13](https://vedabase.io/en/library/bg/2/13/) |
| Disabled persons are less valuable | Soul not reduced; dignity and access required | [BG 2.20](https://vedabase.io/en/library/bg/2/20/) |
| Grief is wrong when someone dies | Distinction teaching misapplied to dismiss bereavement | Facilitator caution |
| Appearance doesn't matter at all | Reduced vanity ≠ ignoring respectful presentation for service | [SB 10.84.13](https://vedabase.io/en/library/sb/10/84/13/) context |
| I can say anything hurtful — it's just bodies | Verbal harm is sinful; bodies feel pain | General Vaiṣṇava ethics |

## Spiritual bypassing red flags

Facilitators interrupt if participants say:

- "I don't need medicine — I'm not the body"
- "Trauma is just māyā — get over it"
- "That person suffers because of past karma" (to their face)
- "Suicide doesn't matter — new body"

Route to private conversation and safeguarding lead.

## Language precision

| Avoid | Prefer |
| --- | --- |
| "I am ugly" | "This body has features I struggle with; I am Kṛṣṇa's servant" |
| "I am my diagnosis" | "I have a body with medical needs; I serve according to capacity" |
| "Bodies don't matter" | "Bodies change; the self continues; care for the instrument" |
