## Key distinctions

- **Deha / dehī** — body vs. embodied knower
- **Changing / enduring** — [BG 2.16](https://vedabase.io/en/library/bg/2/16/)
- **Negation vs. positive identity** — "not the body" points toward "servant of Kṛṣṇa" ([CC Madhya 20.108–109](https://vedabase.io/en/library/cc/madhya/20/108/))
- **Detachment vs. neglect** — care for instrument
