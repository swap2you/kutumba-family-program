## KUTUMBA summary — BG 2.13 London 1973

Śrīla Prabhupāda emphasizes that everyone has direct experience of bodily change from childhood to youth to old age, yet the person remains. This is the beginning of spiritual knowledge — not to identify the self with the temporary body.

## KUTUMBA summary — BG 2.22 London 1973

The garment analogy illustrates that the soul changes material bodies; the example is meant to produce sobriety, not bodily neglect.

## KUTUMBA summary — BG 13.1 London 1973

The body is matter (*kṣetra*); the living entity knows the body. This knowledge is the beginning of distinguishing self from body.
