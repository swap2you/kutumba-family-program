# C1-W2 Presentation Rubric — Gallery & Skits

Friendly feedback for Cycle 1 gallery presentations. Not competitive grading.

## Criteria (1–4 scale)

| Criterion | 1 — Emerging | 2 — Developing | 3 — Strong | 4 — Exemplary |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Distinction clear** | Body/self unclear | Some distinction | Clear change vs continue | Clear + source reference |
| **Source awareness** | No reference | Mentions BG 2.13 | Names verse + link | Verse + humble application |
| **Respect & dignity** | Hurtful content | Neutral | Respectful language | Models inclusion |
| **Creativity** | Minimal effort | Simple completion | Engaging artifact | Memorable + clear |
| **Service link** | No service | Vague | One concrete seva idea | Inspires family action |

## Pass threshold for display

Score ≥2 on **Respect & dignity** required for public gallery. Below 2 → private facilitator conversation.

## Quick feedback phrases

- "I can see what changed and what stayed the same."
- "Thank you for caring for bodies in your pledge."
- "Your diagram helped me remember BG 2.13."

## Facilitator does not

- Rank children against each other publicly
- Critique appearance
- Force presentation for shy children

## Optional self-check (youth)

- [ ] I showed body change without mockery
- [ ] I showed continuing self or service
- [ ] I used kind words about others' bodies
