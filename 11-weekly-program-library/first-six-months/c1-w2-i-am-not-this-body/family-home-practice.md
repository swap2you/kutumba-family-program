## Family Home Practice

| **Field** | **Detail** |
| --- | --- |
| **Minimum** | Use the identity pause once daily at a chosen trigger. |
| **Standard** | Use the pause daily and complete one body-care action that supports service: sleep routine, medication, movement, cleanliness or reduced screen use. |
| **Stretch** | Discuss one appearance or status pressure as a family using BG 2.13. |
| **Evidence** | Private reflection: What changed in my response after the pause? |
