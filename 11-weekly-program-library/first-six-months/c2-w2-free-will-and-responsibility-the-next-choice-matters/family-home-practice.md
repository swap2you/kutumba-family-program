## Family Home Practice

| **Field** | **Detail** |
| --- | --- |
| **Minimum** | Use pause–remember–choose once daily on the chosen trigger. |
| **Standard** | Track trigger, first urge and chosen action for three days. |
| **Stretch** | Repair one poor choice within twenty-four hours through apology, correction or restitution. |
| **Evidence** | Private three-line log; no one must reveal sensitive family conflict. |
