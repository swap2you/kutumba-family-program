## Prem-kī-Kathā Script

*Delivery target: 8–10 minutes of scripted narration plus 5–7 minutes of interaction. Do not exceed the 20-minute block.*

| Two statements often appear in the same home. First: ‘I had no choice; you made me angry.’ Second: ‘I should be able to control everything if I am spiritual.’ Both are inaccurate. We do not control every circumstance, body, emotion, other person or result. But we are not merely helpless machines either. |
