## Bhakti Laboratory — Pause–Remember–Choose Practice

1. Teach a physical pause signal: hand to heart or touching a pocket card.

1. Remember one line: ‘I am Kṛṣṇa’s servant; choose truth and care.’

1. Offer two or three acceptable next actions for each scenario.

1. Role-play once, receive one correction and repeat successfully.

1. For unsafe situations, the correct choice is immediate adult help or leaving—not quiet endurance.
