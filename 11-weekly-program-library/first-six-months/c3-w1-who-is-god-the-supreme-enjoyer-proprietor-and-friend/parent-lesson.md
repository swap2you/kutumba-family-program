## Parent Lesson — 40 Minutes

1. 0–5 min — Possessive-language audit: list common ‘my’ claims in a family without public disclosure.

1. 5–13 min — Read BG 5.29 and identify enjoyer, proprietor, friend and the result—peace.

1. 13–20 min — Compare individual soul, material nature, Supersoul and Supreme Person using a simple four-part diagram.

1. 20–29 min — Case studies: money decision, child achievement, service credit and household authority. Apply the three peace questions.

1. 29–35 min — Stewardship versus neglect: discuss time, body, property and relationships as entrusted resources.

1. 35–40 min — Choose one daily duty and prepare a three-line remembrance before it.
