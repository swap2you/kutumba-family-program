## Teacher Preparation

- Read BG 5.29, 9.10, 13.23, 9.26 and Īśopaniṣad Mantra 1 with purports.

- Prepare the four-part diagram and avoid overcomplicated tattva terminology.

- Select shared materials for a real stewardship task.

- Brief teachers not to use God’s ownership to pressure donations or control personal property.

- Prepare interfaith-sensitive wording: teach KUTUMBA’s siddhānta without mocking other conceptions.
