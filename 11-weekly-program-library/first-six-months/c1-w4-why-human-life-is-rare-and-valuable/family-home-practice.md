## Family Home Practice

| **Field** | **Detail** |
| --- | --- |
| **Minimum** | Protect one fifteen-minute family spiritual appointment. |
| **Standard** | Use the four-step verse method and write one family answer. |
| **Stretch** | Each person brings one sincere spiritual question to the next meeting. |
| **Evidence** | Calendar check and one-sentence reflection; missed appointments are rescheduled, not hidden. |
