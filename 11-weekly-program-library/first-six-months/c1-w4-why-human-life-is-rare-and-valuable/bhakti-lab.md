## Bhakti Laboratory — How to Read One Verse as a Family

1. Display one verse reference and memory line; do not attempt the entire purport with young children.

1. Use the four-step method: hear/read, notice one word, explain in own words, choose one action.

1. Parents practice asking one discovery question and one application question without answering for the child.

1. Families complete a seven-minute trial reading using ŚB 11.9.29 or a child-appropriate summary.
