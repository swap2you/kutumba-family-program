# C1-W4 — Why Human Life Is Rare and Valuable

## Purpose

Teach that human intelligence carries a special opportunity and responsibility for self-realization, while avoiding contempt for animals, panic about death or achievement-based spiritual pressure.

## Cycle

Cycle 1 — first six months (Year 1 foundation)

## Key verse

Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam 11.9.29 — labdhvā su-durlabham idaṁ ... mānuṣyam artha-dam

## Minimum home practice

Protect one fifteen-minute family spiritual appointment.
