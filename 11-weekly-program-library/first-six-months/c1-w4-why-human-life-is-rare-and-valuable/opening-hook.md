## Opening Hook (modern scenario)

_Not Prem-kī-Kathā — see prem-ki-katha.md._

| A family receives a valuable pass that can be used only once. It opens a learning center with guidance and a path to a lasting destination — but they keep it in a drawer, busy with snacks and phones. When the expiry date nears, they panic. The problem was forgetting the pass's unique purpose, not enjoying ordinary responsibilities. |
| --- |

**Delivery:** 2–3 minutes before katha or parent lesson.
