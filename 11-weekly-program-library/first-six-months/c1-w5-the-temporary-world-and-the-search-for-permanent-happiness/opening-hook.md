## Opening Hook (modern scenario)

_Not Prem-kī-Kathā — see prem-ki-katha.md._

| A family plans the perfect holiday — place, clothes, food, smiling photos. Then weather changes, a child becomes sick, the room is noisy, and two people argue. The holiday was not evil. The hidden demand was: 'This arrangement must make us completely happy.' |
| --- |

**Delivery:** 2–3 minutes before katha or parent lesson.
