## Parent Lesson — 40 Minutes

1. 0–5 min — Explain non-competitive rules and the appreciation-only response format.

1. 5–15 min — Mixed-family retrieval stations: verse match, analogy match and misconception repair.

1. 15–30 min — Three-minute family presentations or displays. Maximum four families in this block; remaining families present during lab stations.

1. 30–36 min — Adult case lightning round: appearance crisis, schedule pressure and canceled plan. Each response must include identity + action.

1. 36–40 min — Private continuity decision: start, continue, simplify or stop one home practice.
