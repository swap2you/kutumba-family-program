## Teacher Preparation

- Collect presentation format choices one week earlier; limit preparation burden to twenty minutes per family.

- Set up four stations with answer keys and age adaptations.

- Prepare a visible rule: no ranking, no scores announced, no correction during a child’s performance unless safety requires it.

- Assign timekeeper, station leads and child-transition support.

- Review Cycle 1 misconceptions and prepare concise corrections with references.
