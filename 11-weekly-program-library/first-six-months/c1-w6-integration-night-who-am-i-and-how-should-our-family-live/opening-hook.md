## Opening Hook (modern scenario)

_Not Prem-kī-Kathā — see prem-ki-katha.md._

| A family begins a road journey. One child asks, 'Are we there yet?' Another becomes attached to every rest stop. A third complains the car is not as nice as another family's. The parents forget the map. Integration night is when the family remembers the destination and chooses one practice to continue. |
| --- |

**Delivery:** 2–3 minutes before katha or parent lesson.
