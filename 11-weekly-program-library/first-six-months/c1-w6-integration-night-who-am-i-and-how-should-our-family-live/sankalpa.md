| **Cycle driving question** |

## Cycle outcomes

- Families understand karma without fatalism or victim-blaming.

- Families practice responsible next-choice skills under real constraints.

- Families can discuss reincarnation calmly and compassionately.

- Families recognize the modes as changing influences, not labels.

- Families establish one boundary plus devotional shelter against an attention trap.
