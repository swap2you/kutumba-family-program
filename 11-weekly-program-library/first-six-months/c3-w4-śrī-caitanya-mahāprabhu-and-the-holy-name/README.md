# C3-W4 — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and the Holy Name

## Purpose

Introduce Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu’s gift of congregational chanting and help families experience attentive, humble and joyful nāma practice without performance pressure or claims of advanced realization.

## Cycle

Cycle 3 — first six months (Year 1 foundation)

## Key verse

Caitanya-caritāmṛta Antya 20.12 — Śikṣāṣṭakam 1 — ceto-darpaṇa-mārjanaṁ ... paraṁ vijayate śrī-kṛṣṇa-saṅkīrtanam

## Minimum home practice

Five-minute family kīrtana or attentive mantra listening on three days.
