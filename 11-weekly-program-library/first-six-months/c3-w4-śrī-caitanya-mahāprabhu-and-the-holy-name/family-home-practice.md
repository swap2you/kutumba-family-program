## Family Home Practice

| **Field** | **Detail** |
| --- | --- |
| **Minimum** | Five-minute family kīrtana or attentive mantra listening on three days. |
| **Standard** | Use one cue, one melody and one attention reminder consistently. |
| **Stretch** | One adult or older child practices a two-minute lead under a trained person’s guidance. |
| **Evidence** | Private check marks and one note: what helped hearing? |
