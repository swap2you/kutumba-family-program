## Bhakti Laboratory — Authorized Kṛṣṇa-Kathā Retelling

1. Use a four-card structure: source, event, quality of Kṛṣṇa, family application.

1. Facilitator models a three-minute Govardhana retelling without invented dialogue.

1. Families prepare a ninety-second retelling from supplied source summaries.

1. Listeners identify the quality and application, not performance style.

1. Teacher corrects factual errors kindly after the retelling.
