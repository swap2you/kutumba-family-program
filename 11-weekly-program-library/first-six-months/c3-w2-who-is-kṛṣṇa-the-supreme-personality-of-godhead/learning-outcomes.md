## Learning Outcomes

| **Outcome domain** | **By the end of the session, participants should be able to…** |
| --- | --- |
| **Knowledge** | State that Kṛṣṇa is the source of all spiritual and material existence and that nothing is superior to Him. |
| **Skill** | Support Kṛṣṇa’s supreme position with BG 7.7, BG 10.8 and ŚB 1.3.28. |
| **Practice** | Choose one name, quality or authorized pastime of Kṛṣṇa to remember daily. |
| **Character** | Wonder, affection, theological clarity and avoidance of sectarian aggression. |
| **Child** | Say that Kṛṣṇa is the Supreme Personality of Godhead and the source of everything. |
