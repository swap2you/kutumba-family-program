**Paraphrase (source-grounded):** In the prison of Kaṁsa at Mathurā, at an auspicious moment, the Supreme Personality of Godhead appeared as the son of Devakī and Vasudeva. The atmosphere became peaceful; demigods offered prayers. The Lord appeared in His four-armed form first, then, at the request of His parents, became visible as a human-like child — extraordinary yet approachable.

**Paraphrase (source-grounded):** By the Lord's arrangement, the child was brought to Gokula under the care of Mother Yaśodā and Nanda Mahārāja. Geography is taught carefully: appearance in Mathurā; childhood pastimes in Vraja under parental care.
