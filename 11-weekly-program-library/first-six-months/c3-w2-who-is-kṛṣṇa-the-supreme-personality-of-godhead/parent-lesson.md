## Parent Lesson — 40 Minutes

1. 0–5 min — Opening question: ‘What evidence would distinguish conviction from preference?’

1. 5–15 min — Text triangle: BG 7.7, BG 10.8 and ŚB 1.3.28. Groups summarize what each adds.

1. 15–22 min — Read a brief summary from the Kṛṣṇa Book Introduction on Kṛṣṇa’s supreme identity.

1. 22–30 min — Govardhana case: identify Kṛṣṇa’s supremacy, protection, reciprocation and correction of pride.

1. 30–35 min — Respectful explanation practice: answer a sincere question without attack or vague universalism.

1. 35–40 min — Select one authorized name/quality/pastime for daily remembrance.
