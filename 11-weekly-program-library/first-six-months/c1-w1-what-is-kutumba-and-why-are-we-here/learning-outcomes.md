## Learning Outcomes

| **Outcome domain** | **By the end of the session, participants should be able to…** |
| --- | --- |
| **Knowledge** | State the six purposes of KUTUMBA in simple language. |
| **Skill** | Participate in a short guided family discussion without interrupting, advising or comparing. |
| **Practice** | Complete the first family saṅkalpa and place it visibly at home. |
| **Character** | Humility, reliability, non-comparison, respectful speech and willingness to serve. |
| **Child** | Say, ‘Our family can help one another remember Kṛṣṇa.’ |
