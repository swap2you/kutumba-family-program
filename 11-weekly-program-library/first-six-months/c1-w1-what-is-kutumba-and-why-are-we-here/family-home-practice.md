## Family Home Practice

| **Field** | **Detail** |
| --- | --- |
| **Minimum** | Hold one five-minute family practice before the next meeting: one prayer, three mahā-mantras and one appreciation. |
| **Standard** | Complete the minimum practice twice and place the family saṅkalpa card near the sacred space. |
| **Stretch** | Add one short reading from Bhagavad-gītā or Kṛṣṇa Book and one small family service. |
| **Evidence** | One private check mark per completion; no photo proof or public reporting required. |
