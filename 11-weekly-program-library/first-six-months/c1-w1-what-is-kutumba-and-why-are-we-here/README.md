# C1-W1 — What Is KUTUMBA, and Why Are We Here?

## Purpose

Establish KUTUMBA as a disciplined family sādhana and formation community—not a social club, substitute temple, initiation pathway, or lecture series—and help every family choose one realistic way to grow together.

## Cycle

Cycle 1 — first six months (Year 1 foundation)

## Key verse

Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam 1.2.18 — nityaṁ bhāgavata-sevayā

## Minimum home practice

Hold one five-minute family practice before the next meeting: one prayer, three mahā-mantras and one appreciation.
