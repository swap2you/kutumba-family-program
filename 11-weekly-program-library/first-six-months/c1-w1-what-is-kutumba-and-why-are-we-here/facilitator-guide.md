## Teacher Preparation

- Read ŚB 1.2.17–21 and the KUTUMBA charter sections; prepare a three-minute explanation only.

- Print purpose cards, saṅkalpa cards, age-band activities and family covenant summary.

- Prepare the mock sacred-space materials; confirm that no unapproved worship procedure will be taught.

- Assign children to bands and confirm two-adult visible-space coverage.

- Brief all volunteers: no family comparison, no public disclosure of private practice, no theological improvisation.
