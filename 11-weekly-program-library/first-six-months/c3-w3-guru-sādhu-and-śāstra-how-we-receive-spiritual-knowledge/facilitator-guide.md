## Teacher Preparation

- Read BG 4.34, SB 11.3.21, CC Madhya 20.122 and KUTUMBA authority policies.

- Confirm exact language with the temple liaison for initiation, guru and worship boundaries.

- Prepare source-verification exercises using only authorized texts.

- Review child-protection escalation and explain that misconduct reports are not gossip or offense when handled properly.

- Instruct teachers to say ‘I do not know’ rather than improvise.
