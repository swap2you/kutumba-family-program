## Parent Lesson — 40 Minutes

1. 0–5 min — Source check: participants rank a viral quote, anonymous post, Śrīla Prabhupāda book, trained teacher and personal intuition by authority for doctrine.

1. 5–13 min — Read BG 4.34 and identify humility, inquiry, service and qualified knowledge.

1. 13–21 min — Guru–sādhu–śāstra triangle. Explain harmony and basic role distinctions.

1. 21–29 min — Case studies: charismatic contradiction, teacher speculation, pressure for initiation and a genuine safeguarding concern.

1. 29–35 min — Question clinic: convert vague, combative or dependent questions into specific, text-grounded inquiry.

1. 35–40 min — Choose one question, one source and one qualified person to approach.
