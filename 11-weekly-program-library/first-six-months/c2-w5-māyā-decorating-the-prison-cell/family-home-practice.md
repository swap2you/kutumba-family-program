## Family Home Practice

| **Field** | **Detail** |
| --- | --- |
| **Minimum** | Use one media or consumption boundary for three days. |
| **Standard** | Use stop–name–redirect each time the chosen loop begins. |
| **Stretch** | Complete a twenty-four-hour pause from one nonessential app, purchase or praise-seeking behavior and redirect the time to service. |
| **Evidence** | Private note of trigger, false promise and redirected action. |
