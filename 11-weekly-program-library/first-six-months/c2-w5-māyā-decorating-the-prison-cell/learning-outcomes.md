## Learning Outcomes

| **Outcome domain** | **By the end of the session, participants should be able to…** |
| --- | --- |
| **Knowledge** | Explain māyā as divine material energy that binds through the three modes and makes misidentification and misplaced attraction appear natural. |
| **Skill** | Recognize one attention trap and name the false promise it makes. |
| **Practice** | Create one concrete boundary around media, consumption or recognition-seeking. |
| **Character** | Vigilance, humility, simplicity and dependence on Kṛṣṇa. |
| **Child** | Understand that something can look attractive yet pull us away from what matters. |

## Primary Śrīla Prabhupāda Sources

- Bhagavad-gītā 7.14

- Bhagavad-gītā 2.62–63

- Bhagavad-gītā 15.7

- Bhagavad-gītā 9.10
