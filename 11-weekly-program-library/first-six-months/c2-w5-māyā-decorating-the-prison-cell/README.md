# C2-W5 — Māyā: Decorating the Prison Cell

## Purpose

Teach māyā as Kṛṣṇa’s difficult-to-overcome material energy that covers and misdirects the conditioned soul, while showing surrender, association and devotional practice as the way through—not hatred of the world or of women.

## Cycle

Cycle 2 — first six months (Year 1 foundation)

## Key verse

Bhagavad-gītā 7.14 — daivī hy eṣā guṇa-mayī mama māyā duratyayā

## Minimum home practice

Use one media or consumption boundary for three days.
