## Teacher Preparation

- Read BG 7.14 and 2.62–63 with purports; prepare precise definitions.

- Confirm all teachers will explicitly reject misogynistic misuse of the term māyā.

- Prepare age-appropriate media examples without displaying inappropriate content.

- Avoid public disclosure of addiction or compulsive behavior; offer private support pathways.

- Prepare device-boundary cards and a five-minute chanting plan.
