## Parent Lesson — 40 Minutes

1. 0–5 min — Observe three room images: calm/ordered, overstimulated and neglected. Describe conditions, not people.

1. 5–14 min — Read BG 14.5–9 in summarized form. Build a three-column tendency chart.

1. 14–20 min — Explain mixing and change; discuss how fatigue, media, food, company and time of day influence behavior.

1. 20–29 min — Scenario analysis: study session, temple service, shopping, family argument and weekend sleep. Identify dominant influences and one elevating action.

1. 29–35 min — Goodness trap: pride, comfort attachment and judging others. Connect to BG 14.26.

1. 35–40 min — Redesign one evening routine using remove, add, move earlier and simplify.
