## Learning Outcomes

| **Outcome domain** | **By the end of the session, participants should be able to…** |
| --- | --- |
| **Knowledge** | Name sattva, rajas and tamas and describe their basic tendencies. |
| **Skill** | Identify mode-like influences in an environment, habit or decision without labeling a person. |
| **Practice** | Complete one ‘clarity before chanting’ routine this week. |
| **Character** | Discernment, moderation, orderliness and compassion. |
| **Child** | Identify calm/clear, restless/grabbing and dull/confused patterns in simple situations. |
