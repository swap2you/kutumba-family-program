## Teacher Preparation

- Read ŚB 7.5.23–24 and purport carefully; prepare the traditional example names only if teachers can present them accurately.

- Coordinate station content with the worship lead so no unapproved procedure is taught.

- Prepare icons and short age-appropriate actions for all nine processes.

- Avoid overloading families with nine home commitments.

- Brief teachers that personality preference is not the sole authority for selecting practice.
