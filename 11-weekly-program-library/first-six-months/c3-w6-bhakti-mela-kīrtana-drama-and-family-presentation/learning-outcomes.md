## Learning Outcomes

| **Outcome domain** | **By the end of the session, participants should be able to…** |
| --- | --- |
| **Knowledge** | Connect God’s peace formula, Kṛṣṇa’s supreme position, guru–sādhu–śāstra, the holy name and the nine processes. |
| **Skill** | Present one source-based principle through kīrtana, drama, poster, demonstration or explanation. |
| **Practice** | Create a thirty-day family bhakti baseline with one daily, one weekly and one monthly element. |
| **Character** | Joyful service, cooperation, humility, reliability and gratitude. |
| **Child** | Contribute through a safe role suited to age and comfort. |
