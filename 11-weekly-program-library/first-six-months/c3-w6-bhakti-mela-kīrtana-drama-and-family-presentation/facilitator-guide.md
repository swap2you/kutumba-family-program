## Teacher Preparation

- Collect presentation choices two weeks ahead and impose strict preparation caps.

- Review every script, slide and song for source accuracy and age suitability.

- Confirm kīrtana, worship, child-safety, food and room-flow responsibilities.

- Prepare private assessment rubrics and baseline planning cards.

- Do not add certificates implying formal qualification, initiation readiness or Bhakti Śāstrī credit.
