## Parent Lesson — 40 Minutes

1. 0–5 min — State mela rules: service, source accuracy, time limits, no ranking and child opt-out roles.

1. 5–25 min — Family presentations in rotating zones: source poster, short drama, kīrtana role, nine-process demonstration or stewardship exhibit.

1. 25–31 min — Guru–sādhu–śāstra source check quiz using teams but no public score.

1. 31–36 min — Six-month learning chain reflection: identity, responsibility and relationship with Kṛṣṇa.

1. 36–40 min — Draft the thirty-day baseline and identify mentor support if needed.
