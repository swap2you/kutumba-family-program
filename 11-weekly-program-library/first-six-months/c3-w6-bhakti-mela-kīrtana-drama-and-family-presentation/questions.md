## Questions: Discovery, Understanding and Application

### Discovery

- Which principle from each cycle changed your understanding most?

- Which source can you now locate and explain?

- Which home practice was most sustainable?

### Understanding

- How do identity, responsibility and relationship with Kṛṣṇa depend on one another?

- Why must source authority and safety remain part of joyful bhakti?

- Why is a small baseline stronger than an impressive temporary vow?

### Application

- What will your family do daily, weekly and monthly?

- What is the minimum version during disruption?

- What support is needed, and what remains the family’s own responsibility?
