## Bhakti Laboratory — Bedtime Remembrance Routine

1. Choose a fixed cue: device charging, teeth brushing or entering the bedroom.

1. Sit or stand safely before the sacred space or a clean image.

1. Chant the mahā-mantra once slowly or hear a short approved recording.

1. Name one gratitude and one request for remembrance.

1. End without extending into a lecture or forced emotional sharing.
