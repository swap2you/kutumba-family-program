## Parent Lesson — 40 Minutes

1. 0–5 min — Anonymous question box: participants may submit a question about death or reincarnation without discussion of personal loss.

1. 5–13 min — Read BG 2.13 and 2.22. Identify continuity, change and analogy limits.

1. 13–20 min — Read the memory line from BG 8.6. Explain life-direction and repeated consciousness rather than superstition about one thought.

1. 20–30 min — Response practice: answer a five-year-old, ten-year-old and grieving adult in two or three sentences.

1. 30–35 min — Misconception check: ghost stories, choosing any next body, guessing a deceased person’s destination and minimizing grief.

1. 35–40 min — Design a one-minute bedtime remembrance practice.
