## Teacher Preparation

- Read the assigned verses and prepare only the level needed for foundational teaching.

- Check whether any participating family has a recent bereavement; offer an opt-out or private warning.

- Prepare gentle materials; no graphic images, frightening stories or public testimony requests.

- Brief teachers not to interpret dreams, paranormal claims or a specific person’s rebirth.

- Have a plan to refer intense grief or trauma to appropriate pastoral or professional support.
