## Learning Outcomes

| **Outcome domain** | **By the end of the session, participants should be able to…** |
| --- | --- |
| **Knowledge** | Describe five qualities taught in the assigned texts: eternal, conscious, individual, unchanging through bodily change and a minute part of Kṛṣṇa. |
| **Skill** | Support a statement about the soul with a verse reference rather than imagination. |
| **Practice** | Offer one daily action consciously as service rather than for ego or recognition. |
| **Character** | Dignity, responsibility, service identity and respect for every living being. |
| **Child** | Say that every living being has a soul and should be treated with care. |

## Primary Śrīla Prabhupāda Sources

- Bhagavad-gītā 2.17–25, especially 2.20

- Bhagavad-gītā 15.7

- Bhagavad-gītā 13.23 (distinguishing the Supersoul)

- Śrīla Prabhupāda’s purport to Bhagavad-gītā 2.20
