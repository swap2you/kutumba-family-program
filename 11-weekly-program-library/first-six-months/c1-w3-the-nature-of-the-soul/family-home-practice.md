## Family Home Practice

| **Field** | **Detail** |
| --- | --- |
| **Minimum** | Offer one routine action to Kṛṣṇa daily with a ten-second intention. |
| **Standard** | Complete one unnoticed family service without announcing it, then discuss at week’s end what service identity changed. |
| **Stretch** | Read BG 2.20 together and have each family member choose one word describing the soul. |
| **Evidence** | Private note: duty, intention, obstacle and one improvement. |
