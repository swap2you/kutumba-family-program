## Prem-kī-Kathā Script

*Delivery target: 8–10 minutes of scripted narration plus 5–7 minutes of interaction. Do not exceed the 20-minute block.*

| Last week we learned that the changing body is not the complete self. But merely saying what we are not leaves an empty space. A child may ask, ‘Then what am I?’ Bhagavad-gītā gives a positive answer. The soul is not a temporary blank or a piece of matter. The soul is an eternal, conscious individual. |
