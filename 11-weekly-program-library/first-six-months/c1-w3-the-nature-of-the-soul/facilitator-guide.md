## Teacher Preparation

- Read BG 2.17–25, 15.7 and 13.23 with purports; use exact terminology and avoid monistic interpretations.

- Prepare a safe LED light demonstration rather than open flame for children.

- Check cleaning materials and child-safe task assignments.

- Brief facilitators that service does not justify domestic unfairness, coercion or abuse.

- Prepare glossary cards: jīva, ātmā, Paramātmā, prakṛti and sevā.
