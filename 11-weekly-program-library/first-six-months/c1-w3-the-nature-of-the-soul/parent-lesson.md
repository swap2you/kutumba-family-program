## Parent Lesson — 40 Minutes

1. 0–5 min — Retrieval: in pairs, state one correct and one incorrect conclusion from ‘I am not this body.’

1. 5–13 min — Guided reading of BG 2.20. Mark words describing what the soul is and what the soul is not.

1. 13–20 min — Compare BG 15.7 and BG 13.23: individual jīva, material nature and the Supreme witness. Keep terminology introductory.

1. 20–28 min — Analogy lab: spark/fire, sunray/sun and hand/body. Groups identify what each analogy helps explain and where it can mislead.

1. 28–35 min — Case: a person says, ‘All souls are spiritual, so I do not need authority or service.’ Prepare a text-grounded response.

1. 35–40 min — Service reframing: select one disliked duty and rewrite its intention as service without pretending the duty is pleasant.
