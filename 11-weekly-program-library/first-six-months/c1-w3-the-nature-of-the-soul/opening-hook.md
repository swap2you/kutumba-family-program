## Opening Hook (modern scenario)

_Not Prem-kī-Kathā — see prem-ki-katha.md._

| Last week we learned the body changes like clothes. A child asks at dinner: 'Then what am I?' The table goes quiet. It is not enough to say what we are not — Bhagavad-gītā gives a positive answer. The soul is not a blank or a piece of matter. It is an eternal, conscious individual. |
| --- |

**Delivery:** 2–3 minutes before katha or parent lesson.
