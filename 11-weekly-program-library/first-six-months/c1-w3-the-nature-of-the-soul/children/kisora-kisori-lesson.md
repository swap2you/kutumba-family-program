# Kiśora–Kiśorī Lesson — The Nature of the Soul

**Total time:** 40 minutes  
**Essential question:** What am I positively — beyond 'not the body'?  
**No forced personal disclosure.**

---

| Time | Activity |
| --- | --- |

| 35–40 min | Service or practice commitment + transition |

### Text observation — BG 2.20

KUTUMBA summary (not full purport):

> Soul is unborn, eternal, ever-existing, primeval; not slain when body is slain.

**Observe:** (1) Key terms (2) Promised result (3) One home action
