## Questions: Discovery, Understanding and Application

### Discovery

- What kind of work does BG 3.9 say causes bondage?

- What elements besides the visible act shape its moral and devotional quality?

- What did King Nṛga’s situation show about care and accountability?

### Understanding

- Why is karma not identical to fatalism?

- Why can we not diagnose another person’s suffering confidently?

- How does offering work to Kṛṣṇa differ from merely saying ‘for Kṛṣṇa’ after careless action?

### Application

- Which routine action is most often driven by recognition, anger or haste?

- Who may be affected by that action?

- What specific change in intention or method will reduce bondage?
