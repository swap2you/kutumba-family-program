## Family Home Practice

| **Field** | **Detail** |
| --- | --- |
| **Minimum** | Use the before–during–after method once on a routine duty. |
| **Standard** | Use it three times and note one reaction prevented by greater care. |
| **Stretch** | Repair one small unresolved consequence: apologize, return, replace, clarify or complete. |
| **Evidence** | Private action card; do not disclose sensitive conflicts in the group. |
