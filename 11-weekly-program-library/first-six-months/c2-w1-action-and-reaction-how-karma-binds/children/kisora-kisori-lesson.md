# Kiśora–Kiśorī Lesson — 40 Minutes

## Core (ages 9–11)

- Read BG 3.9 and distinguish stopping work from changing its purpose.
- Discuss why good intention does not excuse dishonest or careless method.
- Challenge the statement: ‘Every suffering person deserves what happened.’

## Challenge (ages 12–14)

- Create an action chain showing direct, indirect and delayed effects of one online post.

## Safeguarding

No forced personal disclosure. Debate stays respectful. Route trauma topics to parents privately.
