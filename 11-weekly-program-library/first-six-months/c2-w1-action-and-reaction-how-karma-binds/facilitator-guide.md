## Teacher Preparation

- Read BG 3.9, 4.16–18 and the King Nṛga chapter; prepare a restrained summary.

- Do not present a complete taxonomy of karma, vikarma and akarma beyond the level supported by the lesson.

- Prepare scenarios that involve ordinary family responsibility rather than extreme tragedy.

- Brief teachers to stop victim-blaming language immediately and calmly.

- Prepare child-safe seed activity and check allergies if soil is used.
