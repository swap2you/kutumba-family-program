## Learning Outcomes

| **Outcome domain** | **By the end of the session, participants should be able to…** |
| --- | --- |
| **Knowledge** | Define karma in this lesson as action and reaction under material nature, not merely fate or punishment. |
| **Skill** | Analyze the intention, method and likely consequence of a family action. |
| **Practice** | Transform one routine duty by performing it carefully and offering the result to Kṛṣṇa. |
| **Character** | Accountability, foresight, honesty and compassion. |
| **Child** | Understand that choices plant seeds and seeds produce results. |
