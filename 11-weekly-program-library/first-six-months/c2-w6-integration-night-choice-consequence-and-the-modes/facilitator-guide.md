## Teacher Preparation

- Prepare at least six realistic cases with no recent family-specific details.

- Create facilitator answer guides with acceptable ranges, not one scripted solution.

- Review prohibited responses: karma diagnosis, victim blame, misogyny, child shaming and deterministic labels.

- Arrange mixed-age stations and ensure youth are not left as sole supervisors.

- Prepare continuity cards and private follow-up list.
