## Bhakti Laboratory — Five-Lens Family Case Lab

1. Lens 1: What action and likely reaction are present?

1. Lens 2: What is controlled, influenced or not controlled?

1. Lens 3: Which mode-like conditions are active?

1. Lens 4: What false promise or attention loop is operating?

1. Lens 5: What responsible action and devotional shelter should be used next?

1. Families present in three minutes; facilitator corrects philosophy after the presentation, not during it.
