## Parent Lesson — 40 Minutes

1. 0–5 min — Explain the five-lens case method: action, choice, conditioning/modes, false promise and devotional shelter.

1. 5–25 min — Family case rounds. Each table receives two cases and prepares a three-minute response.

1. 25–32 min — Cross-table review: another family identifies one strength and one missing lens.

1. 32–36 min — Verse recognition relay without speed ranking.

1. 36–40 min — Select one continuing method and one measurable environmental change.
