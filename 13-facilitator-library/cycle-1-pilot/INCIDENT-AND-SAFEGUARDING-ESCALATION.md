# Incident and Safeguarding Escalation (Cycle 1 Pilot)

## Immediate danger

Contact local emergency services first. Then notify designated safeguarding lead and program director.

## Non-emergency concerns

1. Stop the activity if a child or adult is at risk of harm or humiliation
2. Speak privately with the person affected — do not force public disclosure
3. Record factual notes on paper — **not in public Git**
4. Escalate to named safeguarding reviewer within 24 hours

## Content-related corrections

Theological or factual errors discovered during pilot → log in `CONTENT-CORRECTION-LOG.md` (summary only, no private details).

## Repository rule

This public repository must never contain completed incident forms, contact details, or participant identifiers.
