# Prompt 04 — Cycle 1 Visual Production

## Objective

Produce a finite, meaningful and rights-safe visual pack for each Cycle 1 module. Do not create generic decorative clutter.

## Required minimum per module

For C1-W1 through C1-W6 create, where pedagogically appropriate:

1. one principal concept diagram;
2. one kathā storyboard or event sequence;
3. one age-appropriate analogy/activity visual;
4. one key-verse reference card;
5. one facilitator session/process map;
6. one home-practice card;
7. one Gamma asset-selection sheet.

Integration week C1-W6 may replace the kathā storyboard with a cycle synthesis wall/map.

## Formats

- canonical diagrams: SVG;
- Mermaid permitted for flows where it remains legible;
- render PNG derivatives at practical presentation resolution;
- no raster screenshots of Markdown;
- no text embedded so small that it is unreadable;
- preserve Sanskrit diacritics;
- provide plain-English alternative labels for Lāla–Lālī.

## Module-specific direction

### C1-W1 — Why are we here?

- Naimiṣāraṇya assembly storyboard;
- “distraction → inquiry → authorized hearing → family practice” flow;
- KUTUMBA six-purpose wheel sourced to the charter;
- do not present the charter as a direct scriptural quotation.

### C1-W2 — I am not this body

- life-stage continuity diagram;
- driver/vehicle analogy with explicit limits;
- changing clothes analogy with explicit limits;
- body-care and spiritual-identity balance;
- no contempt for the body or disability.

### C1-W3 — Nature of the soul

- jīva qualities reference map;
- “not cut, burned, wetted or dried” concept card;
- part-and-parcel / sunray analogy with limits;
- avoid depicting the soul as a measurable glowing human miniature unless clearly labeled symbolic.

### C1-W4 — Human life is rare and valuable

- human opportunity decision path;
- Mṛgāri/Nārada story sequence if that remains the canonical kathā;
- “capacity → inquiry → practice → compassion” map;
- avoid degrading animals or implying human bodily superiority as license for exploitation.

### C1-W5 — Temporary world and permanent happiness

- temporary/permanent comparison;
- Dhruva aspiration transformation story sequence;
- pleasure cycle versus devotional fulfillment;
- avoid claiming ordinary family affection is meaningless.

### C1-W6 — Integration

- Cycle 1 learning map;
- family presentation/project workflow;
- six-week memory-line path;
- no competitive ranking.

## Reuse policy

Create a visual only once when it genuinely serves several modules. Register reuse in the asset catalog.

## Gamma integration

Each module’s `gamma/GAMMA-MASTER-DECK-BRIEF.md` must point to exact local asset IDs and paths, not `[IMAGE: placeholder]`.

The three audience decks may use different crops/labels but should share the canonical underlying fact.

## Review

All visuals remain:

- `doctrinal-review-required`;
- `design-review-required`;
- `rights-review-required` where external references exist.

## Deliverables

- actual SVG assets;
- PNG derivatives where required;
- manifests and alt text;
- updated visual plans;
- updated Gamma asset maps;
- `build-evidence/V7-CYCLE-1-VISUAL-COVERAGE-REPORT.md`;
- contact sheet/index Markdown showing all Cycle 1 visuals.
