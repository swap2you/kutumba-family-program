## Non-negotiable boundaries

- Do not build an application.
- Do not bulk-download or commit copyrighted books, purports, PDFs, audio, video, transcripts, or paintings merely because they are publicly readable.
- Public accessibility is not a redistribution licence.
- Store metadata, stable links, exact references, timestamps, original KUTUMBA summaries, and reviewed short excerpts only.
- Use official/archival sources as provenance anchors.
- Treat mirrors, topic indexes, and YouTube channels as discovery or alternate-access sources.
- Do not automatically merge supplementary Gauḍīya sources into controlling ISKCON doctrine.
- Do not infer approval of a teacher, speaker, edition, or institution.
- Human doctrinal, rights, safeguarding, worship, citation, and pedagogy review remains required.
- Keep the repository public as currently directed.
