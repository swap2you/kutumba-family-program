# Prompt 06 — Pilot Quality Gate

## Objective

Audit C1-W2 before scaling.

## Review roles

Simulate separate reviews, while keeping human approval gates open:

1. Source and citation reviewer
2. Gauḍīya doctrinal reviewer
3. Parent-learning reviewer
4. Lāla–Lālī pedagogy reviewer
5. Kiśora–Kiśorī pedagogy reviewer
6. Safeguarding reviewer
7. Copyright and image-rights reviewer
8. Facilitator usability reviewer
9. Gamma/visual production reviewer

## Scoring

Score 0–100:

- source authority and traceability — 20
- doctrinal precision and scope control — 20
- adult pedagogy — 10
- Lāla–Lālī design — 10
- Kiśora–Kiśorī design — 10
- practical family application — 10
- visuals and presentation readiness — 8
- safeguarding and accessibility — 7
- rights and attribution — 5

Pass threshold: 85.

No pass is allowed with any of these critical defects:

- invented quotation;
- unsupported doctrinal claim;
- full copyrighted purport copied;
- unlicensed image committed;
- empty mandatory file;
- unsafe child activity;
- medical, grief, abuse, disability, or identity harm;
- random forum used as authority;
- live lesson overloaded with research dossier content.

## Report

Create:

`17-reviews-and-audits/C1-W2-GOLD-STANDARD-PILOT-AUDIT.md`

Include:

- score;
- critical findings;
- remediation;
- file-level evidence;
- remaining human reviews;
- final pilot verdict.

Only after automated remediation reaches PASS may Prompt 07 begin.
