# Prompt 08 — Age-Band Migration

## Decision

Operational child groups are:

- **Lāla–Lālī:** ages 4–8
- **Kiśora–Kiśorī:** ages 9–14

## Migration rules

1. Preserve historical source documents unchanged.
2. Update derivative weekly materials and current operating indexes.
3. Do not blindly concatenate old 4–6 and 7–9 content.
4. Rebuild activities around the new groups.
5. Add internal differentiation:
   - Lāla–Lālī core: ages 4–6
   - Lāla–Lālī extension: ages 7–8
   - Kiśora–Kiśorī core: ages 9–11
   - Kiśora–Kiśorī challenge: ages 12–14
6. Both groups study the same central truth as parents, with different language and activity.
7. Teens may lead bounded presentation or service tasks but may not become responsible for child safety or discipline.

## Required repository updates

Update:

- child lesson files;
- templates;
- curriculum maps;
- facilitator indexes;
- family-facing indexes;
- materials planning;
- assessment language;
- validation schemas;
- review status.

Create:

`04-children-youth/TWO-GROUP-DELIVERY-ADDENDUM.md`

The addendum must cover:

- rationale;
- grouping;
- developmental differences;
- mixed-age teaching method;
- room/staffing implications;
- activity design;
- behavior support;
- transitions;
- safeguarding;
- teen leadership boundaries;
- review after pilot.

Do not rewrite the authoritative DOCX silently. Mark this as a controlled derivative decision pending formal incorporation in the next source-document version.
