# Prompt 12 — Śrīla Prabhupāda-līlāmṛta Reading Circle

## Objective

Design an optional Wednesday reading circle after the Friday program has stabilized.

## Location

Create:

`11-weekly-program-library/companion-programs/prabhupada-lilamrita-reading-circle/`

## Standard format

Thirty minutes, normally online:

- 2 minutes — opening and settling
- 20–22 minutes — shared reading
- 5–6 minutes — brief realizations or one reflection question
- 2 minutes — close and next reading assignment

## Participation

- parents and children may read;
- no pressure to comment;
- age-appropriate sections may be assigned;
- one facilitator controls time;
- no speculative debate;
- no replacement of Friday curriculum;
- no attendance ranking.

## Copyright

Do not reproduce the book in Git.

Store:

- reading schedule;
- chapter/page references;
- participant-owned-copy reminder;
- short facilitator questions;
- vocabulary;
- source links where authorized;
- reflection log template without personal data.
